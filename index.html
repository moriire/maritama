About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Maritama Logistics, a leading supply chain and third-party services provider that takes pride in our rich legacy of delivering exceptional freight forwarding, custom clearing bond, custom brokerage, sea and air freight transportation, and comprehensive warehousing solutions. With a single-minded commitment to excellence, we are your ultimate destination for all your logistics needs.

As a one-stop-shop company, we offer end-to-end Freight/Logistics and Supply Chain solutions that span across the globe. Whether you require seamless sea and air freight services, expert handling of dangerous goods such as radioactive materials, or efficient transportation solutions, we have you covered. We understand the complexities and challenges of the logistics industry, and our expertise combined with our state-of-the-art infrastructure enables us to provide tailored solutions that meet your unique requirements.

At Maritama Logistics, we recognize that time is of the essence in the world of logistics. Therefore, we have built a reputation for our timeliness and reliability. With our efficient processes, cutting-edge technology, and meticulous attention to detail, we guarantee prompt and seamless delivery of your goods, enabling you to meet your deadlines and exceed customer expectations.
